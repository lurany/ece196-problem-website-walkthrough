Problem

Problem Definition

Our problem:UCSD students who use campus basketball courts do not have a reliable way to know how crowded the courts are before arriving. This can lead to wasted time walking to the courts only to find that there is no space to play, or missing chances to play when the courts are actually open.

Problem Motivation

Our team chose this problem because students often have to gamble whether the courts will be open or crowded. Right now, many students rely on fixed schedules, but those do not provide reliable real time information. We wanted to focus on a common campus problem that affects recreation and convenience.

Current Existing Solutions

Booking a Court

What works:

  • Guarantees a court if planned ahead of time.

What is missing:

  • Doesn't provide the ability to check live occupancy.
  • Doesn't cover basketball courts.

Checking General Open Recreation Hours

What works:

  • Gives a general idea of when the courts are available.

What is missing:

  • Doesn't provide the ability to check live occupancy.
