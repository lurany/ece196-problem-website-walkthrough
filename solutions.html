Booking a Court

What works:

  • Guarantees a court if planned ahead of time.

What is missing:

  • Doesn't provide the ability to check live occupancy.
  • Doesn't cover basketball courts.

Checking General Open Recreation Hours

What works:

  • Gives a general idea of when the courts are available.

What is missing:

  • Doesn't provide the ability to check live occupancy.

Proposed Solution

  • Motion sensors located on each court scan for activity
  • When a person walks near the sensor while using the court, the sensor sends a detection signal to the processor
  • The processor records a timestamp of when the signal was received, and accumulates the detections over a period of time to determine general activity of each court
  • The id and number of courts being used is sent to a live website, where is it displayed for students to check
